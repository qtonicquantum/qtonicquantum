# Security Policy

## Reporting

Email **ciso@qtonicquantum.com**. Please do not open public issues for vulnerabilities.

We acknowledge reports within 5 business days and aim to remediate critical issues within 30 days. Coordinated disclosure preferred.

## Scope

Issues in the content shipped in this repository.

## Out of scope

The Qtonic Quantum production services (qtonicquantum.com), QScout, QStrike, QSolve, and Q-Lab are evaluated separately under engagement.
